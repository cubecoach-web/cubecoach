Solve the corners

You will need to align all the corners in the right place even if the yellow color on the corner is not up:

To place the corners in the correct spot practice this algorithm until you are fluent:
R U'L' U R' U' L

Now to solve the corners find one corner that is already aligned and rotate your cube so the corner that is already in the right place is in left and repeat the algorithm a few times. This will place all the corners in the right place.

Now there is only one step then the Rubik's Cube will be solved!
