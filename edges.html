Lesson 3: 2nd Layer

You will realize that to solve the 2nd layer you will only need to place
the edges in the right place.

You will need to Know these algorithms:
(i): R U R' U'
(ii): L' U' L U

Practice these algorithms until you are fluent.

To solve the edges, first find the edge that does not have the color yellow and then align the edge color eith the center of its colors and determin where the other color wil go. Whether it will go to right or left.

If the color need to go on the right side move the top layer clock-wise (U) and do the righty algoritm then turn the cube to the right side and then do the lefty algorithm.

And If the color is on the left side turn the top layer counter-clock-wise and do the lefty algorithm then turn the cube left side and do the righty algorithm.

Congrats! You have solved the 2nd Layer now there is only one layer left.
