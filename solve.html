Solve the Cube!

This is the final step when all corners are aligned, practice this algorithm:
U R' U' R

Warning: This algorithm is very likely to be messed up so don't be frustrated.

Place the yellow side on front with the first yellow corner do this algorithm until the yellow is on front then rotate the cube so that the next corner piece comes and repeat this algorithm again until solve and repeat this for all unsolved corner pieces. Please Watch the Video Before:

With this the Rubik's cube will be solved. Congratulations for solving your first ever Rubik's Cube!
