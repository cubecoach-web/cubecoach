Lesson 2: Corners

Now with our white cross done we can move onto aligning the white
corner pieces to solve the first layer!

Hold the white cross down and repeat this algorithm:
R U R' U'

Practice this algorithm until you can do it fluently and always remember
that the cross should be at the bottom

Now let's actualy solve the corners!

Now find a corner piece and find where it will be placed, it has 3 colors
one white and other colors could be any and place it on top and should be on your right and do the algorithm until it's solved.

Repeat the algorithm with all corners and the first layer would be solved!

In case you can't find any corner piece but there is still some slots it whould be stuck under,
you can simply solve it by repeating the algorithm mentioned.
