Lesson 1: White Cross

To begin solving the cross, we'll first create a daisy by aligning
all the white edge pieces around the yellow center.
This forms a daisy-like figure.

Let’s understand how to build it step-by-step.

Hold the yellow center facing you and align all the white edge pieces.

This creates the daisy. Then, rotate each white edge piece
twice to move it below its matching center color, forming a perfect white cross.

Congratulations! You’ve just taken the first step
in mastering the Rubik’s Cube.
